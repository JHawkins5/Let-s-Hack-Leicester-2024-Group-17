Welcome!

Honest reviews from real students

Accom review is a website created by students for students with the sole purpose of improving transparency in the student accommodation market.

Often, we find that accommodation reviews are fake, biased, or not easily accessible. Accom Review sets out to solve this problem by providing student residents with a platform to voice their opinions in a clean and fair manner.
